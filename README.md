# 📊 Business Analytics Portfolio: SQL & Power BI 

Welcome to my data analytics portfolio. This repository contains two distinct projects demonstrating my ability to extract, analyze, and visualize data to solve commercial problems.

## 📁 Project 1: DVD Rental Operations Analysis (SQL)
**Goal:** To optimize inventory and understand customer rental patterns for a hypothetical DVD rental business.

* **Tools:** PostgreSQL
* **Key Techniques:** Complex Joins, Window Functions, Aggregations, CTEs.
* **Business Impact:** Identified the top 5% of customers driving 25% of revenue and recommended targeted loyalty programs.

## 📁 Project 2: Bicycle Retail Sales Dashboard (Power BI)
**Goal:** To provide executive stakeholders with an interactive overview of regional sales performance and product profitability.
